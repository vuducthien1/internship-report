# Self-Assessment

**Infor:** Self-assessment during the internship process

Throughout my internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd.** from **April 17, 2026, to July 10, 2026**, I had the opportunity to experience a real-world working environment, practice professional conduct, and apply the knowledge I acquired to researching and practicing AWS cloud computing services.

During the internship, I participated in learning, practicing, and exploring how to use AWS services, thereby gaining a deeper understanding of the cloud platform, cloud system architecture, and the process of deploying real-world solutions. In addition, my team and I built a small cloud project to apply our acquired knowledge into practice, thereby improving technical skills in cloud computing, teamwork abilities, problem-solving mindset, and technical reporting capabilities.

Regarding professional conduct, I always strived to complete assigned tasks, comply with company regulations, proactively learn, and actively communicate with team members to enhance work efficiency. The internship process helped me recognize my strengths to continue leveraging, while also identifying the skills that need improvement in the near future.

### Areas for Improvement

- **Learning Agility:** Need to improve the capacity to absorb new knowledge faster, especially when working with AWS services, technical documentation, and deployment tools in a cloud environment.
- **Communication Skills:** Need to communicate more proactively with the academic advisor, mentor, and team members to articulate problems more clearly and improve work coordination efficiency.
- **Proactiveness:** Need to be more courageous in asking questions, seeking solutions, proposing ideas, and taking on extra tasks when opportunities arise, instead of strictly focusing on assigned tasks.

### Conclusion

In general, the internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd.** provided me with extensive knowledge and practical experience in the field of cloud computing. I have gained a better understanding of how AWS services are used to build, deploy, and operate a cloud system. Concurrently, I also improved my teamwork skills, technical practice skills, and solution design mindset.

In the future, I will continue to practice the limited skills, especially learning agility, communication capabilities, and proactiveness at work, to achieve better personal growth and meet the demands of a professional work environment.
